Why Eat With Us?

Vegan and Gluten-Free

Our menu includes a selection food and drinks which caters for everyone's dietary requirements. We offer vegan paninis, gluten free sandwiches and paninis and all our drinks can be made from oat or soya milk. We also have gluten-free chocolate brownies, flapjacks and cookies.

Delivery New

You can now enjoy your food and drinks from the comfort of your own home with our new delivery service in partnership with Deliveroo, Uber Eats and Just Eat. Simply place your order through the relevant app and your order will be delivered to your doorstep.

Fair Trade Coffee

All of our coffees are made from freshly ground fair trade coffee beans. This is because we are committed to ensuring that the farmers that grow our coffee beans are paid a fair amount. This is so they can invest in growing better quality beans and provide a better quality of life for their families and communities.

Community Matters

We're committed to giving back to the communities which we operate in. Each month we donate a percentage of our profits to community organisations voted for by our customers to support their causes. In the past the funding we have provided has supported schools to purchase new equipment, health and wellbeing groups to run wellbeing activities and aged care charities to provide care for elderley citizens.
